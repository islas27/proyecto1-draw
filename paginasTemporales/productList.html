HW-Race as Administrator

The administrator must check every order made by the costumers and approve or reject each sale, in order to get the order into the final step of selling the items selected by the costumer.

Welcome to the this interface, here you will be able to check the order requests from every costumer. As and admin. you must verify every order made by all our clients, and also approve or reject each order. The way you can approve a sale you will only get a click on the "approve button"s from the order you want to mark as approved and the status will change into "approved" and by now the costumer will be able to buy the items he/she ordered. And if you do not approve the sale, just click on the "rejected" button and by doing this, the costumer won't be able to buy the items.
